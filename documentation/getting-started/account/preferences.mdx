## Overview

Your account preferences help you customize Francis to work exactly the way you need it to. These personal settings apply only to your account, giving you the freedom to create your ideal working environment without affecting your team members' experience.

## Account Settings

### Interface Theme

Choose the visual style that helps you work most comfortably:

- **Light Mode**: Optimized for well-lit environments
- **Dark Mode**: Reduces eye strain in low-light conditions
- **System**: Automatically matches your device's theme settings for seamless integration

### Number Format

Select the number format that matches your regional standards. This setting affects both how you view numbers and write functions in Francis:

|               | US Format (Default)                                                               | EU Format                                                                        |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Numbers**   | Thousands: comma separator<br/>Decimals: period<br/>Example: 1,234.56            | Thousands: period separator<br/>Decimals: comma<br/>Example: 1.234,56            |
| **Functions** | Parameters separated by commas                                                    | Parameters separated by semicolons                                                |

### Zero Display Options

Control how zero values appear in your models with these display options:

- **Dash (-)**: Replaces zeros with a dash for cleaner visualization
- **Blank**: Shows empty cells instead of zeros
- **Zero (0)**: Displays the actual zero value

Your choice only affects your view of the data, allowing you to maintain your preferred format while collaborating with team members.
